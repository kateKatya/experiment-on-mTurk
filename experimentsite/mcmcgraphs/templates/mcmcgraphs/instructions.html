Results

Once you complete the task, you will receive a completion code and feedback about your accuracy in the task. The feedback shows how accurate your guesses were with respect to the actual population growth, and is for your information only.

In order to complete the HIT you will need to paste the completion code into the appropriate box on the HIT launch page.
